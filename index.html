The Challenge

High Failure Rates

Traditional drug discovery faces a 90% failure rate in clinical trials, with billions spent on candidates that ultimately fail. This inefficiency stems from poor prediction of drug behavior in human systems.

Time and Cost

The average time from target discovery to approved drug spans 10-15 years, costing over $2.5 billion. This lengthy process delays critical treatments from reaching patients in need.

Limited Validation

Current approaches often rely too heavily on either computational or experimental methods alone, missing the benefits of rapid empirical validation of computational predictions.
